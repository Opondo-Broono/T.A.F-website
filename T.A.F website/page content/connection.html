Get Connected

We would love for you to be a part of what God is doing at TAF. Two of the best ways you can get connected to the community here are by joining a group or a volunteer team.

Families

We believe that life is meant to be shared and TAF Families are an intentional way for people to do that. Joining a group is a great way for you to connect with others, grow in your faith, and serve the community. Families are made up of 10-15 people and meet weekly to discuss the text and teaching from Sunday.

Join a Family

Volunteer

Without volunteers, the very things we enjoy on Sundays at Epic - coffee, music, lights, words to our favorite songs, programming for our kids - would not exist. Needless to say, volunteers are highly valued here!

There are opportunities to serve within the church as well as the city.
