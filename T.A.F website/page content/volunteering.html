In the Church

Without volunteers, the very things we enjoy on Sundays at Epic - coffee, music, lights, words to our favorite songs, programming for our kids - would not exist. Needless to say, volunteers are highly valued here!

More importantly, by serving in a volunteer team, we follow Jesus' lead to serve. Jesus came to serve (Mark 10:45), and as we serve one another, we use our unique gifts to play a proactive part in our church, the body of Christ (1 Corinthians 12:18-27).

Hospitality

Our purpose is to make everyone who comes to Epic feel welcome. We do this by greeting people at the door, serving coffee, helping people get seated, and welcoming new guests. All you need to be a part of the team is a friendly smile and the heart to help others have great, heart-warming experience at Epic.

Production

This team enhances our worship experience by providing audio and video support during our Sunday gatherings. By using sound, lighting, video, and stage design, you can help everyone see, hear, and feel the music and teaching at Epic every week. No experience necessary; we'll make sure you're fully trained and comfortable in your new role.
